Omaha Trips

The razorbacks have made it to the College World Series in Omaha, NE 11 times total. The best they have finished is runner-up in the 2018 World Series, falling short to Oregon State in game 3 of the Championship. Each time Arkansas plays in Omaha, the entire state of Arkansas seems to make the trip up north. Razorbacks seem to take the city by storm, since they are usually the closest team.

Recent Dominance

Arkansas has 45 or more wins in each of the last 5 full seasons(2020 was not completed due to COVID-19). They have 7 total years of 45 or more wins. Arkansas has won the SEC West 3 out of the last 4 seasons. Recently, they have proven to be one of the best programs in the country.
